Job Posting Web Application

QuickBuck

Project Overview

QuickBuck is a comprehensive web application designed to empower fresh graduates and undergraduates in the competitive job market. It provides a platform for them to showcase their skills and talents, connect with potential employers, and find freelance and entry-level job opportunities.

Project Goal

The goal of QuickBuck is to bridge the gap between recent graduates and employers by:

  • Offering a centralized platform for job seeking, talent discovery, and professional networking.
  • Empowering graduates to build their portfolios and demonstrate their abilities.
  • Enabling employers to discover promising new talent and streamline the recruitment process.
    Features:
  • User Registration and Login: Users can register and choose their role as either a job seeker or job provider.
  • Role-Based Access Control (RBAC): Users have access to specific functionalities based on their role (job seeker or job provider).
  • Job Board: Graduates can search for and apply to freelance and entry-level job opportunities. Employers can post job openings and manage applications.
  • User Profiles: Graduates can create profiles to showcase their skills, experience, and educational background. Job providers can create company profiles to provide information about their organization and job opportunities.
  • Portfolio Building: Job seekers can add their skills and their education in order to showcase their background and knowledge.
  • Virtual Wallet and Subscription Service: A virtual wallet system with Stripe integration allows users to pay for a subscription service that provides additional benefits within the app
  • Real-Time Chat: A chat functionality enables job seekers and job providers to communicate directly, enhancing opportunities for both parties.
  • Job Bookmarking: Job seekers can save interesting job postings for later reference.
  • Job Search and Filtering: Job seekers can search and filter job postings based on various criteria, such as date (newest or oldest) and keywords.
  • Job Applications: Job seekers can easily apply for job openings.
  • Applicant Tracking System (ATS) for Job Providers: Job providers can view applications, access candidate CVs, and search for specific candidates based on usernames.
  • Company Profiles: Job seekers can view company profiles to learn more about potential employers, their work culture, and current job openings.
  • Job Provider Dashboard: A dashboard allows job providers to manage their company profile, edit and delete job postings, and track applications.

    Technical Specifications:
  • Front-End Development:Angular 17
  • Back-End Development: ASP.NET Core 6
  • Security:User authentication with JWT (JSON Web Token) based authorization to restrict unauthorized access.
  • Payment Gateway:Stripe integration for secure online transactions.
